How to play the game

Try to cross the platform and reach safety on the lily pads. You can use the arrow keys to navigate and press 'J' to leap two spaces. Every time you reach a lily pad, you get 50 points and start again at the beginning. Collect as many points as possible before losing all 3 lives.

The viruses are deadly, don't let them touch you. The masks might save your life if you can hang on but be careful not to fall off or you might DIE.
